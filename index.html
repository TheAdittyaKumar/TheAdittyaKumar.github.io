Toothless - Scout Robot

Toothless is an advanced Arduino-powered robot designed to detect fire, water, and harmful gases while navigating tough terrains. It's built for disaster response, featuring autonomy with ESP32 and GPS integration.

View Project

Using natural poly glutamic acid and Moringa oleifera seeds as coagulants for water treatment.

In response to water pollution in Bangladesh, we developed an affordable and sustainable water purification method using naturally produced poly-glutamic acid combined with Moringa oleifera seeds as a coagulant. This approach addresses the needs of rural areas, providing an eco-friendly alternative to expensive purification techniques.
